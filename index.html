Environment... In the meaning of this word, it is necessary to invest not only the concepts of atmosphere, biosphere, hydrosphere and lithosphere. No. The environment is the living world that surrounds us, in which we grow, from which we learn to live. Nature is the same living being as we are, which can also love, protect or take revenge for an inappropriate attitude towards it. "Even stone has a soul..."
